Our Agreements / Our Commitments

1. Pre-Shoot Preparation (3 Days Before)
  • Confirm shoot date, time, and location.
  • Share shot list and script outline.
  • Discuss desired B-roll shots for storytelling.
  • Review equipment checklist (cameras, mics, lights, etc.).
2. Day of the Shoot
  • Arrive on time and set up equipment.
  • Rehearse intro/outro sequences.
  • Capture talking head videos as planned.
  • Film B-roll with movement in mind.
3. Post-Shoot
  • Review footage together and discuss what worked well.
  • Provide feedback on framing, composition, and storytelling.
  • Assign a rating for overall performance and note areas for improvement.
4. Continuous Improvement
  • Discuss new ideas for incorporating more dynamic shots.
  • Explore different locations or techniques for future shoots.
  • Regularly update this checklist to refine our process.
